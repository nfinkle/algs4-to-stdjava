PrintWriter (Javadoc API) works almost identically to Out (Javadoc API). Note that StdOut is a special case of Out, where Out is initialized to the System.out stream.

The only difference between Out and PrintWriter is that Out flushes all output immediately, while PrintWriter buffers the output until a newline character is added before flushing the output.
